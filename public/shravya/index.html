Volunteering Certificate: Shravya Shruti

To Whomsoever It May Concern

Ms. Shravya Shruti has been volunteering with There Is No Earth B since March 2021. During her time here, she has contributed her time and effort in different domains and has added value to the organisation. 

She joined TINEB at a time when the organisation’s work was focused on Covid relief work. Along with the team, she enabled us to reach and aid 4.84 lakh people – helping them access doctor’s consultation, medication, hospital facilities, homemade food, and more.

Apart from Covid relief work, she has also given tangible research inputs to the documents and reports that TINEB developed. She displays good logical and analytical skills in her writing, and her writing style is lucid and coherent which makes it easily accessible to more readers.

She also volunteered her graphic designing skills to create graphics for our social media content, regularly. These social media posts have helped TINEB expand its subscriber base and keep them engaged with meaningful and aesthetic content.

Ms. Shruti displays a high level of professionalism and commitment in all her tasks and has consistently produced timely deliverables par excellence. She has an aptitude for quick on-the-job learning, and grasps new concepts well. She has the capability to organize and manage multiple tasks simultaneously. Her innate curiosity to learn new concepts, ideas, and approaches, was clearly visible in the attitude she displayed towards work assigned to her.

We particularly appreciate Ms. Shruti’s team spirit and her willingness to help her teammates without reservations. She would be a valuable asset to any team or organization. We strongly vouch for her credentials and recommend her as a candidate for this programme.

Sincerely,
