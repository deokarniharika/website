Subject: Letter of Recommendation for Ms. Punya Dhamija

To Whomsoever It May Concern

Ms. Punya Dhamija has been volunteering with There Is No Earth B since December 2019. During her time here, she has contributed her time and effort in different domains and has added value to the organization.

During her time here, she has contributed her time, effort, and skills to There Is No Earth B’s various campaigns and initiatives around climate change awareness, waste management and sustainable living.

Punya co-coordinated and participated in multiple waste clean-up events organized by TINEB, collecting and responsibly disposing close to 200 kgs of non-biodegradable waste around New Delhi, India. These clean-ups raise awareness about proper waste management among citizens and draw the authorities’ attention towards poor waste management practices in the city. She also actively contributed to the planning and execution of various environmental campaigns, which were citizen-led advocacy movements to demand stoppage of harmful and unregulated industrial and destructive activities being conducted by government and corporate interests in the forests of India.

Punya also actively worked on ideating, creating, and posting relevant and aesthetic content about TINEB’s work on its social media pages. These social media posts have helped TINEB expand its subscriber base and keep them engaged.

Punya displays a high level of professionalism and commitment in all her tasks and has consistently produced timely deliverables par excellence. She has an aptitude for quick on-the-job learning and grasps new concepts well. She has the capability to organize and manage multiple tasks simultaneously. Her innate curiosity to learn new concepts, ideas, and approaches was clearly visible in the attitude she displayed towards work assigned to her.

We particularly appreciate Ms. Punya’s team spirit and her willingness to help her teammates without reservations. She would be a valuable asset to any team or organization. We strongly vouch for her credentials and recommend her as a candidate for this program.

Sincerely,

There Is No Earth B
